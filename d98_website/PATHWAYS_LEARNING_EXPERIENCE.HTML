Learn how to start your journey of personal and professional development through Pathways, Toastmasters’ new education program!

Pathways is an experience that will help all of us in District 98 and especially in India, to take our Toastmasters Learning Experience to the next level. This is the enhancement that we all wanted and waited for. The changing Business and skills needed to lead teams in organizations and societies, have driven the Pathways Program to incorporate projects that would focus on getting what we need, with flexible and innovative learning solutions without disturbing the current Toastmasters Club Meeting structure. Pathways features online learning, cutting-edge content, interactive tools, strong mentoring and evaluation components, and a multitude of options.

To get started, login to the Toastmasters.org website with your personal ID, then go to Education > Pathways Learning Experience

What is Your Educational Journey?

The Toastmasters Pathways learning experience offers many different routes for exploration and achievement.

This modernized learning experience is the result of years of planning and expertise by a large team, including the Board of Directors, World Headquarters staff and member volunteers. The program’s wide-ranging benefits include:

  • Customized learning tailored to personal and professional goals
  • Early and frequent recognition of accomplishments
  • Mobile access to educational materials
  • Expanded video and digital content

Progress in Toastmasters learning is a journey of self-development. Pathways will help you reach your potential.

District 98 is one of the few districts to launch pathways program in April 2018.
